Submit a Correction

If you notice any incorrect meeting listings please report them to us here we will do our best to correct them. It may take up to a month for us to make amendments to the database. We will send you an email to confirm we are looking into the changes when you submit the form below.

We need enough information to find the meeting you want corrected. The name, email, telephone, address, time, day and details fields are required.

For venues which have more than one meeting on different days and times you will need to submit multiple corrections for each meeting.

Please make sure you give the exact details of what you want updated in the details box and enter a valid email so we can reply to you.
