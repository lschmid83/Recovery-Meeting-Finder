Donations

This website is not for profit. The only costs we have to cover are minimal hosting fees apart from that all donations will be divided between the fellowships and donated directly to local homegroups. Thank you.

A.A.'s Seventh Tradition states: “Every A.A. group ought to be fully self-supporting, declining outside contributions.” This means that only A.A. members contribute financially to A.A. – and even A.A. members are limited in the amount they can contribute.

Please only donate if you are attending AA, CA, NA or OA meetings.

Visit our Crowdfunder page if you would like to donate.
